The Budding Techie 2022-23 is a competition to showcase the web development skills of students of classes 6, 7, 8 and 9 from
Isha Vidhya Schools in Chittoor, Viluppuram, Erode, Nagercoil, Dharmapuri, Vanavasi, Cuddalore, Thuthukudi, Karur and Coimbatore.

This competition was held at the end of a ~3 month long course titled
Introductory course in web development (HTML & CSS) (February 2023 - April 2023).

1st Prize - Smartphone worth Rs. 7,999

2nd Prize - Active Noise Cancelling Headphones worth Rs. 4,099

3rd Prize - Remote Control Drone worth Rs. 2,699

On top of this, all participants who submit a valid project get a participation prize!

What should I do to participate in the competition?

  1. First register to participate in the competition by submitting this form
  2. Once you have registered, work on creating a website on any of the topics listed below.
  3. Once you are satisfied with your project, submit your project with this form before the deadline

When is the deadline?

31st May 2023

Submission Guidelines

  1. You must create a website using what you have learned so far.
  2. Deploy your website files to Netlify and submit a project link. Watch this video to learn how to deploy site to Netlify. You'll need to be on a computer for this.
  3. For 1st, 2nd and 3rd Prizes - Submit your project link before deadline. Whoever scores the highest number of points for the projects will win. See the project evaluation criteria below to see how points will be awarded for the projects.
  4. For course participation prize - Submit a link to a valid project link by the deadline.

Project Topics

You have to create a website as your final project. You can pick one of the following options or choose your own kind of website to build:-

  1. Website for a school
  2. Website for a photographer
  3. Website for a shop keeper
  4. Website to show tourist places in your region
  5. Website for your team
  6. ***Your own Idea!***

Project evaluation criteria

After you submit your project, it will be graded and given points (out of a total of 60 points) based on the following criteria:-

  1. Overall presentation (10 points) - How good the project feels on the whole, if it feels cohesive with proper design choices.
  2. Complexity of features (10 points) - No. of pages in your website, how much content, how many varieties of features
  3. Creativity (10 points) - How realistic your website looks, use of media (images/videos/audio clips/animations)
  4. Learning to learn (10 points) - Bonus features (use of features not taught in the course)
  5. Originality (10 points) - How unique is your project?
  6. Code quality (10 points) - Formatting of code, no repetitive code, fewer lines of code, proper comments

The project with the maximum points will win the competition. In case of a tie, the winner will be decided based on the score on the leaderboard app.
